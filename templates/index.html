This software was developed at the Hackunamatata hackathon in the Haag (October 17th-19th 2014).

The code is MIT licensed and on github, of course.

This tool was developed by Dan Palmer (@danpalmer), Chris Hunt (@thisisthechris), Christopher Gutteridge (@cgutteridge).
